Emotion Detection 🛡️

Utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze the user's current emotional state through input devices or manual selection.

Dynamic Playlist Generation🚀

Automatically generates and updates playlists based on the user's mood and preferences.

Sleep Assistant 📈

A dedicated mode that plays soothing music designed to improve sleep quality, with a timer to automatically turn off playback.

Interactive Mood Journal 🦉

Allows users to track their mood and music listening history, offering insights into how music affects their emotional wellbeing.

Social Sharing and Playlists ✨

Enables users to share their mood-based playlists with friends or discover playlists created by others within the MoodMelody community.

Mood Transformation Challenges 🧰

Challenges that encourage users to shift their mood through guided music listening sessions, such as "From Stress to Calm in 30 Minutes".
